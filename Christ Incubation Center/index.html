Introduction

Industry Institute Interaction Cell (IIIC) has been incepted to provide the students a real-time industry experience through various projects and case studies from the repositories of industry to ensure they are well prepared. This evolution combined with intensive practical and applied research experiences through industry oriented curriculum (IOC) provides a wider platform for students to get internships through “FastTrack” program that would ensure students are placed. The cell also inculcates the need skill develop program through certification programs for the students for innovation through applied research and product development in niche areas of Data science, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Design Thinking, Cyber Security Internet of Things and Network Security. We also mentor and support the incubation ideas while providing infrastructure and financial help based on the mutual agreement.

The IIIC is a cell that would be one point contact for all industry based connects in any form while providing technical knowledge, applied research, design thinking and inspiring students to take part in industry oriented projects via innovation and research.
